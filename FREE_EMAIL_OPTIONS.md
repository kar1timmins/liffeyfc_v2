# Free Email Service Alternatives (No SMTP)

## Option 1: EmailJS (Recommended - 200 emails/month free)

### Setup:
1. Go to [EmailJS.com](https://www.emailjs.com/)
2. Create free account
3. Add email service (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
4. Get Service ID, Template ID, and Public Key

### Benefits:
- ✅ No SMTP configuration
- ✅ Works directly from frontend
- ✅ 200 emails/month free
- ✅ No server changes needed
- ✅ Reliable delivery

## Option 2: Formspree (1000 submissions/month free)

### Setup:
1. Go to [Formspree.io](https://formspree.io/)
2. Create free account
3. Get form endpoint URL

### Benefits:
- ✅ Simple form submissions
- ✅ 1000/month free
- ✅ No backend changes
- ✅ Spam protection included

## Option 3: Web3Forms (Unlimited free)

### Setup:
1. Go to [Web3Forms.com](https://web3forms.com/)
2. Get access key
3. Simple form submission

### Benefits:
- ✅ Completely free
- ✅ No signup required
- ✅ Simple integration
- ✅ No limits

## Option 4: Netlify Forms (100 submissions/month)

### Setup:
1. Deploy to Netlify
2. Add `netlify` attribute to form
3. Automatic handling

### Benefits:
- ✅ Built into Netlify
- ✅ 100/month free
- ✅ No configuration
